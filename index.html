Mindset

mindset is a set of beliefs that shape how you make sense of the world and yourself. It influences how you think, feel, and behave in any given situation. It means that what you believe about yourself impacts your success or failure

How to start Mindset?

  1. believe in yourself
  2. change your perspective
  3. set goals
  4. start dreaming
  5. keep going!

How to practice Mindset?

  1. look for a new hobby
  2. estabilish a daily routine
  3. be active
  4. work on your self-talk
    (remember: you're the most important)
  5. find some time alone
  6. love yourself
